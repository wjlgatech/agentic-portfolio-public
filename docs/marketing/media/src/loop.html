Loop engineering, applied to marketing

The closed viral loop

Machines do the drafting, reviewing, measuring, learning. A human owns the Send button.
1 · MakeAUTO
Draft the artifact + per-platform copy, grounded in real work — no invented facts.
↓
2 · ReviewAUTO
Score vs a rubric: hook, specificity, honesty, CTA, limits. Iterate until it clears the bar.
↓
3 · Human gate🔑 ALWAYS HUMAN
You read it, you click Publish. Nothing ever auto-posts, auto-DMs, or auto-replies.
↓
4 · Publish + engageASSISTED
1-click share intents; replies to every comment are drafted for you — you send.
↓
5 · MeasureAUTO
One ledger: impressions, replies, makes, K. Computed from real numbers, never vibes.
↓
6 · LearnAUTO
What hooked, what flopped → banked as rules the next draft must obey. The loop compounds.
⟲ back to 1 — every post starts smarter than the last
